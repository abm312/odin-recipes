Chicken Biryani Recipe Baby

Description

Chicken Biryani is a delicious and aromatic rice dish made with marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices. Perfect for a special occasion or for a casual dinner with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice until it is 70% done.
  4. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot and cook on low heat for 30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
