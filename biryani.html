Chicken Biryani Recipe Baby

Description

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice. For my Biryani, I simplify the order of operations, while retaining the traditional layered approach to assembling it. This recipe captures the essence of how I like to make it, with a few of my own touches. Itâ€™s the perfect dish to make for a special occasion or for a casual dinner with friends and family.

Ingredients

      Chicken
      Basmati rice
      Onions
      Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice until it is 70% done.
  4. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot and cook on low heat for 30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
